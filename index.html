About us

Born out of a passion for sport and a love for coaching, VPA burst onto the scene in 2013 as a tech startup developed by a young sport science graduate looking to provide a development pathway for talented junior athletes in his local sporting community.   From learning how to operate a basic camera, to comprehensive research into the current landscape of technologies surrounding sport and coaching in Australia- Luke and VPA's growing team of young entrepreneurs had their work cut out for them.   VPA now encompasses a range of core products, services and technologies that operate within a developing sport and broadcast landscape.   The team provides professional and complete technology solutions such as live streaming and production services, online management and content distribution systems, venue and facility infrastructure installations for broadcast AND analysis, as well as integrating new technologies focusing on the emerging landscape of digital content commercialisation.   Check out VPA's clients and services page to find out more and don't forget to reach out via the contact page if anything here excites you.
